3500 cm²
3500 cm² is a project aimed at promoting contemporary artworks, curated by Lorenzo Benedetti. 3500 cm² represents the square centimeters available to an artist to create a 50×70cm poster, with the goal of bringing contemporary art to a wider and more diverse audience compared to those who usually visit exhibitions. The posters are distributed for free and disseminated across the territory in an open and democratic manner.
