3500cm² Expodemic at Palazzo delle Esposizioni

The 3500cm² Expodemic project is currently featured at the prestigious Palazzo delle Esposizioni in Rome. This initiative highlights the transformative power of art in times of crisis, presenting a collection of works that examine the effects of epidemics on society, culture, and human connection.

EXPODEMIC – Festival of Foreign Academies and Cultural Institutes

07 May – 25 August 2024

Curated by Lorenzo Benedetti in collaboration with Francesca Campana.

The exhibition is promoted by Rome's Cultural Affairs Department and the Azienda Speciale Palaexpo. It is organized in conjunction with major institutions, including the Accademia di Belle Arti di Roma, Villa Medici, British School at Rome, American Academy in Rome, and many others.

Artists: Kamrooz Aram, Ane Rodriguez Armendariz, Séverine Ballon, Jacopo Belloni, Alix Boillot, Susanne Brorson, Fatma Bucak, Pedro Luis Cembranos, Zachary Fabri, Hamedine Kane, Kapwani Kiwanga, Bjørn Melhus, Marko Nikodijevic, Tura Oliveira, Estefania Puerta Grisales, Chloé Quenum, Marie Robert, Sarina Scheidegger.

Roma Expodemic

The Roma Expodemic project explores the intersection of art, public health, and community engagement. Through a series of exhibitions, workshops, and site-specific installations across Rome, the initiative sheds light on the impact of epidemics on urban spaces and human relationships. It brings together local and international artists to spark dialogue and promote a collective reflection on resilience and recovery.
