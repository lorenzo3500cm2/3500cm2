Roma Expodemic

The Roma Expodemic project explores the intersection of art, public health, and community engagement. Through a series of exhibitions, workshops, and site-specific installations across Rome, the initiative sheds light on the impact of epidemics on urban spaces and human relationships. It brings together local and international artists to spark dialogue and promote a collective reflection on resilience and recovery.

Exhibition at the Italian Consulate in Paris

The Italian Consulate in Paris hosted a remarkable exhibition showcasing works from emerging and established Italian artists. The event highlighted the vibrant cultural exchange between Italy and France, focusing on contemporary interpretations of identity, heritage, and migration. This exhibition aimed to strengthen diplomatic ties through the universal language of art.
