Expodemic at Palazzo delle Esposizioni
7 May - 25 August 2024
The Expodemic project explores the transformative power of art during crises, showcasing works from international academies and cultural institutes in Rome. The exhibition will be held at Palazzo delle Esposizioni.

12 March - 15 May 2024
A unique exhibition highlighting the connection between Italian and French cultures through contemporary art. The event will take place at the Italian Consulate in Paris.
Roma Expodemic
Ongoing
